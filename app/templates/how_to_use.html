How to Use The Ward

A practical study workflow for ADN nursing students — aligned with a traditional four-semester program and NCLEX-RN preparation.

Student Guide

1. Start with Your Semester

Open the Dashboard and review the ADN Curriculum Map. Each semester links directly to the module that matches your current coursework — Fundamentals and Microbiology in Semester 1, Dosage and Assessment in Semester 2, Med-Surg and Mental Health in Semester 3, and NCLEX Prep in Semester 4.

You do not need to complete modules in order, but following the curriculum map keeps your study aligned with clinical rotations and exams.

2. Study Inside Each Module

Every module follows the same teaching-first pattern:

  • LearnRead concepts, disease processes, or reference material. Click Verify Source to see textbook and NCLEX citations.
  • PracticeWork through NCLEX-style questions with rationales. Read the explanation even when you answer correctly — that is where the teaching happens.
  • DrillUse scenario drills (red flags, break-the-chain, complications) to build prioritization and clinical judgment.
  • FlashcardsFlip cards, rate them Know / Need Review, and revisit weak areas. Progress saves in your browser automatically.
  • ExportDownload Markdown or printable sheets for offline review before exams.

3. NCLEX Prep Strategy

Use the NCLEX-RN Prep Center once you have covered at least two content areas. Start with By Topic to focus on weak categories, then switch to Mixed Practice to simulate exam conditions. After each session, review missed questions in Review Missed — those IDs are stored in your browser until you answer them correctly.

Pay attention to the Clinical Judgment notes under each rationale. They map to the NCSBN Clinical Judgment Model steps shown on the NCLEX page.

4. Recommended Weekly Rhythm

During the semester
  • 20–30 min: active module for current class
  • 10 min: flashcards from the same module
  • 5 min: one dosage calculation (if in NURS 145)
Before exams / NCLEX
  • 25-question NCLEX mixed session
  • Review Missed questions until clear
  • Export cheat sheets for final review

5. Navigation Tips

  • Ctrl+K — open the command palette to jump to any module or tab
  • Module tabs support deep links — bookmark /modules/assessment.html#flashcards for quick access
  • On mobile, use the menu icon to open the sidebar; tabs scroll horizontally on small screens
  • Install the site as an app (PWA) from your browser for faster access and offline shell caching

6. What Gets Saved

The static site stores flashcard ratings, NCLEX missed-question lists, and custom terms in localStorage on your device. Clearing browser data will reset progress. Export study materials if you need a permanent copy.
